Step 1

Registrer & request quotes

Set up your user ID and password, then answer a few questions about your home. We aren't looking for an interrogation, just a few questions such as when your home was built and any updates that could provide discounts to your quote.

Step 2

Review quotes as they return

The quotes from your local insurance agents will soon begin flowing into the Insure Beacon hub. As they return, you will be notified by email and SMS alerts. This ensures you only check in when you know a quote is available.

Step 3

Select your policy

Lastly, once you have enough quotes to make an educated decision, you can request contact from the local insurance agent. They will help you finalize the policy and even assist in canceling your existing coverage.
